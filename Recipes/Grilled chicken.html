Grilled Chicken

DESCRIPTION

This simple, sensational marinade and sauce for grilled chicken is made with

lemon, garlic, rosemary, and butter. Separate the marinade into thirds: 1/3 for

marinating, 1/3 for basting, and 1/3 for topping.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup fresh rosemary
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 (6 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a food processor, blend butter, rosemary, garlic, lemon zest, and lemon juice
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Cook chicken breasts on hot grill, basting with rosemary and lemon basting
